Visit Us in Parañaque City!

We are located in the heart of Parañaque City, Metro Manila. Come and enjoy a cup of coffee in a cozy atmosphere!

Getting Here

Our coffee shop is easily accessible by public transportation. Here are some directions:

  • By Car: Take the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and exit at Bicutan. Follow the signs to Parañaque City.
  • By Bus: Take a bus heading to Parañaque and get off at the nearest bus stop. We are just a short walk away.
  • By Jeepney: Ride a jeepney that goes to Parañaque and ask the driver to drop you off at our location.
