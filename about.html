Our Story

At But First, Coffee, we believe in the power of a great cup of coffee. Our journey began with a passion for quality and community.

Founded on March 13, 2023, we have been serving our customers with the finest coffee sourced from the best beans around the world. Our baristas are trained to craft the perfect cup, ensuring that every sip is a delightful experience.

We are not just a coffee shop, we are a community hub where friends gather, ideas are shared, and memories are made. Join us for a cup of coffee and be part of our story!

But First, Coffee isn’t just a café—it’s a vibe. It’s the place where early mornings feel softer, meetings get a little more creative, and friendships are brewed slowly, one cup at a time.

But it’s not just about the coffee, it’s about the experience. Our café is designed to feel like an extension of your living room, with cozy seating, inviting decor, and a playlist that feels like your favorite record spinning in the background. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick caffeine fix, settling in with your laptop, or catching up with friends over pastries, you’re always welcome here.
