Common Questions

1. What are your opening hours?

We are open from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday to Sunday.

2. Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a selection of vegan and gluten-free pastries available. Please ask our staff for more details.

3. Can I book a table for a large group?

Yes, we accept reservations for groups of 6 or more. Please contact us in advance to secure your booking.

4. Do you have Wi-Fi available?

Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for all our customers. Feel free to bring your laptop and enjoy your coffee while you work!

5. Do you sell gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards that can be purchased at our shop or online. They make a great gift for coffee lovers!

6. Can I bring my pet to the café?

We love pets! Our outdoor seating area is pet-friendly. However, pets are not allowed inside the café.

7. Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery through popular food delivery platforms. Check our website for more details on delivery options.

8. What kind of coffee beans do you use?

We use high-quality, ethically sourced beans from around the world. Our beans are roasted fresh and brewed to perfection.

9. Do you offer any loyalty programs?

Yes! We have a rewards program where you earn points with every purchase. You can redeem them for discounts, free items, and special offers.
