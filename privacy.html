Your Privacy Matters to Us

At But First, Coffee, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Our goal is to be transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information to ensure that your experience with us is both enjoyable and secure.

Information We Collect

To provide the best service possible, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: We collect details such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other personal information you provide when you interact with us (e.g., signing up for our newsletter or reaching out for support).

Protecting Your Data

We take your privacy seriously. We have implemented strong security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your trust is important to us, and we strive to keep your data safe at all times.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and other tracking technologies to improve your experience. These help us remember your preferences, track usage, and provide a more personalized experience. You can manage cookie settings through your browser, and opt to accept or decline them as you wish.
