Our Sustainable Practices

At But First, Coffee, we are passionate about making a positive impact on the environment. We believe that sustainability is key to the future of our planet, and we are committed to reducing our ecological footprint through a variety of practices.

How We Practice Sustainability

Ethically Sourced Coffee

We partner with farmers who prioritize fair trade and sustainable farming practices. Our coffee beans are sourced responsibly, ensuring that both the environment and the farmers thrive.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

All our products are packaged with sustainability in mind. We use biodegradable and recyclable materials to reduce waste and make a positive impact on the environment.

Waste Reduction

We are committed to reducing waste by composting coffee grounds, recycling materials, and repurposing products. Every small action adds up to a big difference.

Community Involvement

We actively participate in local environmental initiatives, from tree planting to supporting community clean-up events. Together, we can make a difference!

Join Us in Making a Difference!

Sustainability is a journey, and we invite you to join us in our mission to make a positive impact. Bring your reusable cup to any of our locations and enjoy a discount on your drink. Together, we can help reduce waste and promote a more sustainable future!
