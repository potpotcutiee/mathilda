Building Connections

At But First, Coffee, we believe in the power of community. We strive to create a welcoming space where everyone can come together to enjoy great coffee and connect with one another.

Community Events

We regularly host events that bring our community together, including:

  • Open Mic Nights: Showcase your talent or enjoy performances from local artists.
  • Coffee Workshops: Learn about brewing techniques, coffee tasting, and more.
  • Charity Drives: Join us in supporting local charities and initiatives.

Local Partnerships

We are proud to partner with local businesses and organizations to support our community. Whether it's sourcing ingredients from local farms or collaborating with artists for our events, we believe in supporting our neighbors.

Get Involved

We invite you to join us in our community efforts! Whether you want to participate in our events, volunteer, or simply spread the word, your support makes a difference.

For more information on upcoming events or how to get involved, feel free to contact us at info@butfirstcoffee.com.
