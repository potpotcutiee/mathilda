Join Our Faithfulness Program!

At But First, Coffee, we value our customers and want to reward you for your faithfulness. Our Faithfulness Program is designed to give you great benefits every time you enjoy your favorite coffee with us.

How It Works

Joining our Faithfulness Program is easy! Here’s how it works:

  • Sign up for our Faithfulness Program at the counter or through our website.
  • Earn points for every purchase you make. For every ₱2 spent, you earn 1 point.
  • Once you reach 100 points, you can redeem them for a free drink of your choice!

Exclusive Benefits

As a Faithfulness member, you’ll enjoy!

  • Exclusive discounts on select menu items.
  • Early access to new products and seasonal offerings.
  • Invitations to special events and tastings.
  • Birthday rewards: Enjoy a free drink on your special day!

Sign Up Today!

Don’t miss out on the benefits of being a loyal customer! Sign up today and start earning points on your next visit. For more information, ask our staff or contact us at info@butfirstcoffee.com.
