Accessibility Commitment

At But First, Coffee, we believe that everyone should enjoy their coffee experience without barriers.

Website Accessibility

We are committed to making our website accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 and continuously monitor and improve our website's accessibility. We aim to ensure that all users can easily access content and navigate the site, regardless of their ability or technology used.

  • Our website includes text alternatives for images to aid screen reader users.
  • Navigation is simplified to allow easier movement through the site with keyboard or screen readers.
  • We provide sufficient color contrast to make text readable for people with visual impairments.
  • We ensure all interactive elements are accessible with a keyboard.
  • We aim for compatibility with assistive technologies like screen readers, speech recognition software, and other adaptive tools.

Physical Accessibility

We recognize that accessibility extends beyond our digital presence. All our locations are designed to be fully accessible to individuals with mobility challenges. This includes:

  • Wheelchair accessible entrances and paths.
  • Accessible seating options within the caf√©.
  • Restrooms that meet the accessibility requirements for all patrons.
  • Parking spaces reserved for customers with disabilities close to entrances.
  • Clear signage with visual and tactile guidance to help people with visual impairments navigate the store.

Inclusive Service

We strive to create an inclusive experience for all of our customers, regardless of their background or needs. Our staff is trained to assist customers with diverse needs, ensuring that everyone feels welcome and valued. We offer:

  • Staff members trained in assisting customers with various disabilities, including mobility impairments and visual impairments.
  • Communication aids for those who require them, including sign language interpreters upon request.
  • Menus available in braille and large print upon request at select locations.
  • Assistance with ordering and carrying items for individuals with disabilities who may need help.

Continuous Improvement

We are dedicated to ongoing improvements in our accessibility efforts. We regularly review and update our website and physical locations to ensure compliance with the latest accessibility standards. We also value feedback from our customers and are open to suggestions for improving the experience for everyone.

Our Promise

At But First, Coffee, we believe in the power of inclusivity, and we promise to always prioritize accessibility in our service and offerings. We will continue to improve, learn, and listen to the needs of our customers.
